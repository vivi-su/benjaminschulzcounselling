Payment Policy

  • In-Person Session: Payment is due at the end of each session and can be made by cash, debit, Visa. ( If you are paying by cash please bring exact change. ) After payment is received, a receipt will be issued by email.
  • Vedio Session: Prepayment is required before vedio session. We accept payment through credit card. After you create your confidencial account through the booking system, you will be able to pay your session online.

Online Booking System Security

We take privacy and security very seriously. Our online booking System is HIPAA ( Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act ) Complaint, as well as PCI ( Payment Card Industry Data Security Standard ) Compliant. We use the same level of security as your bank to transfer data, securing your privacy.

Insurance

EXTENDED HEALTH INSURANCE BENEFITS: MSP does not cover our counselling services, but some individuals may have extended health benefit plans that do. It is important to check with your insurance provider as to which counselling services they do cover, which specific clinician and degree, and to what yearly limit.
